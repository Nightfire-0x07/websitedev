What does DevilSec offer?

1 on 1 Mentoring

DevilSec can provide hands on training from senior level members, some of whom already work in the info sec industry.

Intro to Linux and Command Line

Most of the staff use Linux as their daily driver OS and can provide support for members just starting to learn the ropes of Linux and using the terminal.

Project Based Learning Opportunities

DevilSec continues to grow and add new approaches to breaking into the infosec and cybersecurity industries. Staff typically need support when it comes to building CTF infrastructure, educational materials, and other web based technologies. There are new projects every year and all members are welcome to join projects and learn along the way.

Guest Speakers

We find industry professionals to give presentations on their projects, work, and how they got into their fields. These talks provide opportunities for Q&A and getting advice on networking and bolstering your professional portfolio

Networking

Whether you are a virtual participant or an in-person member, DevilSec includes working professionals who regularly post job openings and internship opportunities. On campus events provide a way to introduce yourself and get more involved while virtual meetings and discussions can help other members get to know you better and offer advice based on your interests.

Community

Our group enjoys learning and teaching new tools, playing Hack the Box as a group, and just chatting in voice channels in Discord. We encourage a welcoming atmosphere so anyone at any level regardless of their background can join and start learning at their own pace while having access to support when they need it.
