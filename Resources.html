Getting Started

Cybersecurity encompasses so many areas that it's hard to know where to start. Here are some of our recommendations for learning and practicing the basics when it comes to hacking the web and beyond!

Where to practice

Over the Wire

A free series of wargames that help you learn and practice security concepts in successive stages that build on each other.

 Hack the Box

A challenging platform that offers free and vulnerable machines to scan, explore, and hack in a CTF style.

 Port Swigger

A free web security based platform that includes a web academy to learn almost every concept regarding website hacking and practice in vulnerable labs. Burp Suite is the main tool used for these labs, and there is a free community edition for learning purposes.

 Try Hack Me

Free training platform that has individual rooms to learn each security concept as it relates to web, cryptography, Windows, Linux, and software like Wireshark, Hydra, and Nessus. Similar to Hack the Box, but it is considered easier and better for beginners.

 Cyber Defenders

A training platform with a focus on defending networks from malicious attacks.

 Blue Team Labs

A training platform in defensive techniques related to security investigations.

Where to learn Linux

Linux Journey

An easy-to-follow guide on everything you should know about Linux that comes with helpful examples to reinforce your knowledge.

 Linux Command

Focuses mainly on terminal commands, shell scripts, and learning to get a handle on optimizing your experience using Linux.

Stay in the know

OWASP Top Ten

The top 10 categories of vulnerabilities found on websites. This is an updated document used by organizations to assess risks and implement changes to prevent these types of vulnerabilities from being found and exploited.

 Krebs on Security

A popular security focused blog by Brian Krebs. Frequently updated with detailed news of hacks, information disclosures, and leaks.

 Malwarebytes Blog

Good general information blog about all things related to cybersecurity, reports, privacy, and news on software patches.

 Bleeping Computer

General computing blog that includes cybersecurity, tutorials, deals on paid training platforms and software, and forums.

Recommended reading

The Five Pillars

A detailed layout of getting into the infosec field and clear explanations of the different roles available in the industry.

 How to become a hacker

An older but consistently revised document on getting into the "hacker" mindset and learning necessary skills to contribute to solving problems.
